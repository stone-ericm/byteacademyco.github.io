Colophon

Our font choice is Liberation Mono.

"Liberation" represents our departure from the official Byte Academy home page. The official page is filled with information like how to apply to our bootcamp and our tuition costs, but as instructors we need a page highlighting the thing we most care about: what our students have learned to build.

"Mono" means monospace, or fixed-width. Text editors such as Vim and Sublime traditionally display code in a fixed-width font. As programmers, we love monospace.
