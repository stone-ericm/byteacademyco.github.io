Cody Hess

After graduating from Columbia University in 2006, Cody pursued a career as a performer for ten years. Ultimately unsuccessful, he returned to his first love: programming.

He learned Java in college, but his first self-taught language was Perl. He loves Vim and the command line. Lately he enjoys working with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript on the front-end.

Cody is the lead developer of Byte Dev, Byte Academy's online learning platform.
